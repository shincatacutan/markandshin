How to Get to There (from Manila)

By Public Transportation

Ride a Victory Liner in Cubao or Pasay, along Edsa and take any Olongapo, Iba or Sta. Cruz bound bus. More importantly, ride those taking the SCTEX route for faster trip.
From the bus stop, hail a blue jeepney. Alternatively, you can ride a tricycle to the resort. Both options take just about 15-20 minutes. Tell the driver to drop you at Subic Grand Seas resort in Barretto.

By Driving

There are two routes going to Subic Bay via the rural road will take approximately 3 hours of travel time. There is also an alternative path that will cut your travel time in half. Travel via NLEX - SCTEX, this will only take you about 1.5 - 2 hours from Cubao. Kindly check the map or the waze link above for more detailed direction.

Your safety is our concern and no body wants a violation ticket. Please resist the temptation and drive safely. 100KPH only.
